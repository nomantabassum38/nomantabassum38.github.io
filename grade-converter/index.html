Methodology

How it works

01

Percentile calculation

Your grade is expressed as a percentile within your grading system's range — from the worst possible score to the best.

02

Modified Bavarian formula

The percentile is mapped using the standard formula: 1 + 3 × (1 − percentile), placing your grade on the German 1–5 scale.

03

German grade output

The result is rounded to two decimal places, matching how German universities report grades on transcripts.

German Grade  =  1  +  3  ×  (1 − Percentile)

This formula is used by the anabin database and recognized by the German Rectors' Conference (HRK).
